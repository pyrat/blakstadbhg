## A Year Full of Memorable Moments

The kindergarten year at Blakstad is packed with traditions, celebrations and activities that bring children, parents and staff together — and that children carry in their hearts for life.

## Annual Traditions

### Lucia Performance
The Pre-school Department has a long tradition of a **Lucia performance** at Formfin Furniture, and a visit to Hundeidvik private school to see the dress rehearsal for the Christmas show. One of the most cherished traditions in the kindergarten.

### Christmas Party
The whole kindergarten comes together for a big Christmas party in the gymnasium — with performances, food and a wonderful atmosphere. Parents are warmly welcome.

### Carnival
The children dress up and celebrate with fun and activities. We use healthy alternatives like fruit salad and smoothies instead of traditional cake.

### UN Café
We mark UN Day with our own café in the gymnasium — a great opportunity to learn about the world and different cultures.

### Film Screenings
The kindergarten uses the gymnasium for its own film evenings and screenings throughout the year.

### Solid Start Sykkylven
The kindergarten actively participates in **Solid Start Sykkylven** — a programme to give all children the best start.

## Science Days

Since autumn **2018** we have held **Science Days** where children:

- Explore how things work through experiments
- Ask questions and wonder together with adults
- Work scientifically on topics drawn from nature and everyday life

Science Days are for everyone, but primarily aimed at the pre-school department.

## Seasonal Activities

| Season | Traditions |
|--------|------------|
| **Spring** | Easter celebration · Seed sowing in the vegetable garden · Hiking day to the shore |
| **Summer** | Swimming in Tuvatnet · Sea swimming · Berry picking |
| **Autumn** | Harvesting from the vegetable garden · UN Café · Science Days · Halloween |
| **Winter** | Lucia · Christmas party · Carnival · Ski day and sledding trip |

## Parent Involvement

We greatly appreciate parents who want to contribute and engage. Through the **Parent Council** and the **Cooperation Committee**, parents have real influence on the kindergarten's daily life and development.
