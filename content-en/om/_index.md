## A Kindergarten at the Heart of Nature

Blakstad Nature Kindergarten is an expanded kindergarten with two departments, based in the former school buildings in Tusvik. The kindergarten focuses strongly on **mastery**, **healthy lifestyle** and **outdoor play in nature**. With spacious premises, varied outdoor areas and engaged staff, the kindergarten offers an inspiring environment for children aged 0–6 years.

## Our Departments

### Toddler Department (0–3 years)
Room for **12 children** aged 0–3 years. A safe and stimulating environment where the youngest children develop at their own pace, with a set outdoor time in the mornings and varied activities tailored to the youngest.

### Pre-school Department (3–6 years)
Room for **18 children** aged 3–6 years. We place great emphasis on nature-based learning, with a minimum of two hours outdoors every day — and more when weather and season allow. A dedicated hiking day each week.

## Facilities

The kindergarten is based in the former school buildings in Tusvik and has large, functional rooms, including:

- **Own gymnasium** actively used for events such as Solid Start Sykkylven, UN café, Christmas party, carnival and film screenings
- **Vegetable garden** with potatoes, carrots, sugar peas, rhubarb and an apple tree
- **Large, varied outdoor areas** with hilly terrain, rock faces and natural grounds with forest

## Outdoor Area and Local Environment

The outdoor area is large and varied, with hilly terrain that provides challenges and inspiration for play and activity. The kindergarten is close to excellent hiking areas:

- **The trail around Lake Tuvatnet** — perfect for day trips and nature observation
- **The forest** — where children study wildlife, insects and plants
- **The shore** — for catching crabs and collecting shells
- **Langhol farm** and **the wilderness shelter in Tudalen**
- **The football pitch** next door — which in winter becomes a sledding hill and ski slope
